What is Swap Token?
Swap Token ICO is a Blockchain based application which allows an Initial Coin offering. Here, you can buy Swap Token using your Ethers. You just have to send your ether and you will earn some Swap Tokens in return.This Swap Token is built using Ethereum Smart Contract. It follows the ERC-20 standard and is a valid cryptocurrency which can be exchanged with ethers.

How to Buy?

It just costs 0.001 Ether. You can send your ethers to earn Swap Tokens.

It's Very Simple!!!

  • Click on the Buy Token.
  • Enter the amount of Tokens you want to Buy.
  • Confirm your Transaction.
